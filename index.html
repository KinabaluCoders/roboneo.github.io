RoboNeo

RoboNeo is an open platform and open to all, robotics competition organised by:

  • Ministry of Resource Development & IT
        Sabah State Government
  • Politeknik Kota Kinabalu (Academia)
  • Kinabalu Coders (community NGO)

Introduction

The first-ever Roboneo was held at Suria Sabah in 2014 (Oct 11-12) themed Robo Go Green. The successf of this event sparked a groundswell of interest in even more robotics-related actitivies. our tagline this time around in 2015 is Robo Connect, reflecting our desire to connect enthusiasts in robotics and related disciplines throughout Sabah. Together, we will:

  • Harness the creativity of innovative minds
  • Identify potential products and ideas
  • Bring together like-minded communities
