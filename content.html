RoboNeo

RoboNeo is an open platform and open to all robotics competition
organized by Ministry of Resource Development and Information Technology (State Government),
Politeknik Kota Kinabalu(Academia) and Kinabalu Coders (community NGO)

Introduction

The first RoboNeo event was held at Suria Sabah on 11 and 12 October 2014 with the them Robo Go Green.
The event has sparked interest in the community to continue activities related to robotics.
For 2015, the tagline chosen is Robo Connect. This year we hope to connect more enthusiasts in robotics
be it engineers, students, makers, hackers and even school kids in the state.

The aims and objectives of RoboNeo are:

  • To harness the creativity and innovative minds of the community through robotics
  • To identify potential products and ideas
  • To bring together likeminded communities with the applications of IT, electronics, mechanical and engineering
